# Scenario 01: Knowledge Understanding

category: knowledge_understanding

## User Profile

A 29-year-old product manager wants to understand cognitive psychology to make better product decisions. They have read scattered articles but lack a system.

## Initial Input

"我想系统学习一下认知心理学，感觉它对产品设计和用户理解很有帮助，但我不知道从哪里开始。"

## Expected Skill Chain

1. `goal-clarification`
2. `level-assessment`
3. `learning-map`
4. `path-planning`
5. `resource-curation`
6. `tutoring-coach`
7. `review-adjustment`

## Acceptance Focus

- Convert vague interest into a target such as "4 weeks to understand and apply 8 core cognitive psychology concepts to product analysis."
- Assess through concept familiarity and a product case explanation.
- Map concepts to use cases, not a textbook chapter dump.
- Require a small product-analysis output as evidence.

## Failure Signals

- Recommends a long book list before goal clarification.
- Uses academic jargon without application.
- No evidence task connecting psychology to product work.
