# Scenario 08: Research Exploration

category: research_exploration

## User Profile

A founder wants to understand the humanoid robotics industry enough to decide whether to explore a business opportunity.

## Initial Input

"我想研究一下人形机器人行业，但这个领域太大了，我不知道怎么开始，也不知道怎样算研究明白。"

## Expected Skill Chain

1. `goal-clarification`
2. `level-assessment`
3. `learning-map`
4. `path-planning`
5. `resource-curation`
6. `tutoring-coach`
7. `review-adjustment`

## Acceptance Focus

- Define research decision goal and final output such as an opportunity memo.
- Assess prior knowledge of robotics, supply chain, market, AI, and business model.
- Map research questions, source types, synthesis milestones, and decision criteria.
- Plan must include written synthesis, not endless reading.

## Failure Signals

- Produces a generic market research checklist.
- No decision criteria.
- No synthesis artifact or source quality rule.
